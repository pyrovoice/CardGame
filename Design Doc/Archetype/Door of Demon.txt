Archetype: Midrange
Gimmick: A door with 4 locks. Each lock represents a game action. When all locks are unlocked, the door opens and lets out a demon. Each opening makes the next demon stronger, then the door locks again with 4 new randomized locks. The 4rth opening creates a very strong demon

Locks: - Sacrifice a creature
- Destroy a creature by spell
- Destroy a creature by Combat
- Draw X cards in a turn
- Win combat at a location
- Silence a creature
- Boost a creature
- Control no creatures
- Control X or more creatures
- Have X cards in the graveyard


Cards: - Pick a new lock to switch an existing with
- Unlock a random lock
- 

Boss: Should be pretty polyvalent to help meet the conditions
