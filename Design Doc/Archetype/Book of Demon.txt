Archetype: Control
Gimmick: Game actions write a book, every new action adding a page. When the book is full, it can be cast for a powerfull effect.

Actions: Conquer location
Destroy creature in Battle
Destroy creature by spell
Lose all creatures during a turn
Control more than X creatures
Silence a creature
Destroy a non-creature permanent
Reach turn X
Lose all shield
Lose a life
Cast from extra deck
Card created card

Cards: Add a page marker to the book, which upon casting will complete a random empty chapter
