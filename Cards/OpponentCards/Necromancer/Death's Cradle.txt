Name:Death's Cradle
Types:Artifact,Legendary
CardText:When played, attach to a location.
On location conquered: If lost, banish all creatures at this location and all graveyards, then create a Massive Amalgam token in hand. If won, banish all creatures in the Necromancer's graveyard and create a Lesser Amalgam token in hand.
While at a location: The player does not lose life when losing this location.
